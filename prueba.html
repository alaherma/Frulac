A World In Motion

Our talented in-house team use their wealth of knowledge and ingenuity to service produce for some of the biggest and brightest clients across the globe. Whether we’re working with global brands, international directors or national agencies; making huge TV campaigns or branded online content, our Scottish hospitality and expertise shines through.

Motion

Let Us Set the Scene

We’re the only UK Service Production Company to have an in-house library of over 120,000 images of filming and shoot locations. Country estates, bars, modern architectural properties, industrial warehouses and deep Scottish valleys; we have it all. With expertise in budgeting, filming locations, location scouting and management, casting, and sourcing everything else you need, we provide an exceptional and bespoke service production.

02

Locations

Snap Shot

Over the years we’ve welcomed many fantastic brands to the UK; utilising our shoot location library and production expertise to service some fantastic stills campaigns. From casting real people on the Scottish Isles, to creating snow on a summer’s day in New York, we accomplish every aspect. Our focus is global, but it goes without saying that we know Scotland better than anyone else. In our years of business we’ve covered every inch of the country.

Stills

Film Cars

We own a film cars agency providing vehicles to the film, TV and advertising industries. Our specialty is classic cars, but we have everything from emergency and military vehicles, to modern (or “day to day”) cars and motorbikes. And that’s just for starters. If you need a prop car for your shoot - or would simply like to register your own vehicle - then visit the Film Cars website.
